We showcased how multitouch technology is going to change the way we interact with machines. Thanks to [NUIGroup](http://www.nuigroup.com/) community, we could showcase some applications done by Seth Sandler and Lawrence Muller at [Apogee 2009](http://www.bits-apogee.org/) - held annually at BITS Pilani. In addition to this how to make a Flash App from scratch - with TUIO data (OSC implemented) sent by Touchlib.
