Lechal

Haptic Feedback Based Insole I/O System

Lechal (ले चल) started as a personal project to build a haptic navigation aid for the visually impaired. After 6 months of initial demos, Krispian and I decided to pursue Lechal further as a venture. After several iterations, Lechal has evolved as a full-fledged gait analysis and feedback platform.

Lechal initial prototypes and design got the MIT TR35 Award. The unobtrusive design of Lechal is its most significant feature. The mechanism collects sensor data, learns gait patterns and condenses geographical navigational information, and lets the user feel the directional and proximity information through haptic stimulation.
